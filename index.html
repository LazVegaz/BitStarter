FAQ

Where does all the money go?
Because we've been aiming to record an EP this year for the last 6 months, we have saved up some of our own money already to contribute towards the recording, which is why we are not asking for the full amount required for this project. We want to prove that we are a driven independant band with a plan in sight, we just need a bit of help to make that plan come to life sooner, or else we wouldn't be able to do this for a long time! To be brief, the funding we require goes towards the following things: Studio time, paying the producers, paying the engineers, mixing, mastering, post-production time, EP artwork, manufacturing, marketing and promoting!
